Preach360 Product Messaging Pillars and Features
Pillar 1: Sermon Prep Roadmap
Product features:
The 4-hour process: A sustainable, step-by-step workflow designed to take you from a blank page to a finished sermon without sacrificing your family or burning out.
The PPGR blueprint: A repeatable framework that structurally anchors every message in the clear redemptive progression of Principle, Problem, Gospel, and Response.
Roadmap progress tracker:  A clear, step-by-step visual indicator that keeps your sermon prep moving forward, eliminating guesswork so you stay on pace.
Light and dark mode: An interface designed to reduce eye strain and fit your environment, whether you are working early in the morning or reviewing notes in the evening.
Podium mode: A distraction-free, easy-to-read presentation view designed specifically for the pulpit.
Pillar 2: Theological Depth
Product features
Exegesis guide: A tool that helps you produce an in-depth exegetical analysis of the text from a redemptive framework.
The Relevance Bridge: The conextural connection between the original recipients and modern listeners that reveals the present day need for the text.
Expository mapping: A visual pathway that turns your exegetical study into a homiletical outline.
Outline mode: A workspace that condenses your full manuscript into a clear pulpit outline.
Worldview and Moral Framework Analysis: A diagnostic tool that helps you identify the cultural narratives and false saviors competing for your congregation's hearts, allowing you to effectively dismantle them with the truth of the gospel.
Pillar 3: Optimization and Evaluation
Product features:
Adience optimization: A refinement tool to ensure your language and illustrations connect the grace of Jesus directly to the specific issues and struggles for the unique issues your congregation is facing.
Time/length optimization: Easily expand or shorten your manuscript to fit your allotted presentation time window.
Manuscript optimization: A final polishing step to enhance clarity, tighten wording, and clarify gospel focus for a confident, conversational delivery.
